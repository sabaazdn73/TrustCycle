TrustCycle

The Common Challenge

A tedious cycle of requesting and waiting for recommendation letters is frequently observed. It is understood that academic endorsements are often trapped in manual, repetitive processes.

The Universal Approach

With TrustCycle, an endorsement is requested once and utilized for any objective—be it academic admissions, professional career opportunities, or personal goals. The recommendation is cryptographically verified and kept under full user control.
