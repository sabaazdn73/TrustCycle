TrustCycle

Verified Academic Endorsements on the IOTA Rebased L1

One Verified, Multiple Verifiers

Pilot Phase: IOTA Testnet

This pilot version is currently deployed on the IOTA Testnet. Academic identity and email domains are rigorously verified via Google API integration to ensure institutional authenticity.

Endorsement Workflow

Supervisors are invited to issue endorsements by providing institutional credentials. An OTP sent to an academic email is required to validate the process. Each recommendation is cryptographically secured on-chain, linked to a user-provided identifier (e.g., student ID or passport), and remains permanently under the supervisor's control for potential revocation. Recommendations are accessible via a shareable link for infinite, flexible usage.

Platform Features

A dedicated student dashboard is provided, allowing the management and retrieval of all issued endorsements based on verifiable identifiers. For those wishing to explore the mechanism without actual academic credentials, a demo version is available where OTPs are rendered on-screen.
